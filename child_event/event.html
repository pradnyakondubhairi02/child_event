National Child Day Art Contest


Submission deadline: November 12, 2022

The Office of the Child and Youth Advocate (OCYA) is hosting an art contest to celebrate National Child Day on November 20 and promote the rights of children! Use your voice and say something about your rights and what is important to you!

Children can participate by completing the National Child Day colouring sheet or creating a picture, painting, poem, or short story (up to 350 words).



National Child Day 2022 Webinar on: Stress, Stigma and Safety Supporting Youth to Experience their Right to Safety

In celebration of National Child Day, Agnes Chen, Registered Nurse, Founder of Starlings Community will be hosting a free online event on November 21, 2022, 11:00 A.M. – 12:30 P.M



Turn The World Blue



Landmark buildings around the world will light up blue on the day to show support for child rights. Wear something blue, change your profile picture online and help raise awareness.
